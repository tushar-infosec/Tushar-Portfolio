01 — About

Background

I'm a driven cybersecurity practitioner with a deep focus on offensive security and red team operations. My approach centers on thinking like an adversary — understanding attack methodologies, identifying weaknesses in systems and networks, and providing actionable intelligence to strengthen defensive postures.

I have hands-on experience conducting penetration tests, vulnerability assessments, and exploit research through platforms like TryHackMe, CTF competitions, and dedicated lab environments. I'm proficient with industry-standard tools including Metasploit, Burp Suite, Nmap, and Wireshark.

My goal is to contribute to Red Team, SOC, or Offensive Security roles where I can apply my skills to protect organizations from evolving cyber threats. I continuously invest in learning — staying current with the latest CVEs, attack techniques, and MITRE ATT&CK frameworks.
